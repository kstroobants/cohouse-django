Our Story

Building a Foundation for Cohousing Connections

At Cohouse, our story is rooted in a deep passion for cohousing and a belief in the power of community. We began this journey with a vision to transform the way individuals find their ideal cohousing home and create a platform that fosters vibrant, connected, and sustainable living environments.

It all started when our founder, a cohousing enthusiast and advocate, recognized the need for a centralized and comprehensive resource that would streamline the search process for cohousing communities. Fueled by the desire to make cohousing more accessible and to support individuals in their quest for a meaningful and fulfilling living experience, our founder embarked on the mission of creating a revolutionary cohousing website.

Drawing on their own personal experiences and the collective wisdom of the cohousing movement, our founder assembled a dedicated team of like-minded individuals who shared a common vision. Together, we set out to create a user-focused platform that would provide a wealth of information, resources, and tools to empower individuals on their cohousing journey.

Through countless hours of research, collaboration with cohousing communities, and feedback from residents, we fine-tuned our website to cater to the unique needs of those seeking cohousing homes. We carefully curated a comprehensive network of cohousing listings, ensuring a diverse range of options to suit various preferences and lifestyles.

As we continue on our journey, we remain committed to promoting sustainability and eco-consciousness within cohousing communities. We actively seek out and support communities that prioritize environmental stewardship, energy efficiency, and sustainable practices, aiming to contribute to a greener and more sustainable future.

Our story is a testament to our unwavering dedication to the cohousing movement and the belief that cohousing has the potential to revolutionize the way we live. We are driven by the stories of individuals who have found their sense of purpose, fulfillment, and lifelong connections within cohousing communities. Their journeys inspire us to continually improve and evolve our platform, ensuring that we provide the most comprehensive resources and support to individuals embarking on their cohousing adventure.

Join us in our story as we strive to be a trusted companion, empowering you to find your ideal cohousing community, forge meaningful connections, and embark on a transformative journey of vibrant community living. Together, let's build a future where cohousing becomes a cornerstone of sustainable, connected, and fulfilling lives.
