About Us


Revolutionizing the Way You Find Your Ideal Community

At Cohouse, we are passionate about cohousing and its transformative power to create vibrant, connected, and sustainable communities. Our mission is to revolutionize the way individuals find their ideal cohousing home by providing a comprehensive platform that simplifies the search process and empowers informed decision-making.

We understand that finding the perfect cohousing community is not just about finding a place to live but discovering a way of life that resonates with your values, aspirations, and desire for meaningful connections. That's why we have created a website that goes beyond conventional listings, offering a wealth of resources and tools to help you navigate the world of cohousing.

What Sets Us Apart:

  1. Extensive Cohousing Network: Our website brings together an extensive network of cohousing communities from around the country. We have meticulously curated listings to ensure that you have access to a diverse range of options, enabling you to explore different styles, sizes, locations, and community dynamics. No matter your preferences, we strive to provide choices that suit your unique needs.
  2. User-Focused Experience: We prioritize user experience and have designed our website with your needs in mind. Our intuitive search interface allows you to easily filter and narrow down your options based on specific criteria, such as location, amenities, sustainability practices, and more. We believe that finding your ideal cohousing community should be a seamless and enjoyable process, and we are dedicated to making that a reality.
  3. In-Depth Community Profiles: Making an informed decision about your future home requires comprehensive information. That's why we provide detailed community profiles that delve into the essence of each cohousing community. From their core values and mission statements to their unique features, decision-making processes, and resident testimonials, our profiles offer an authentic glimpse into the community's culture, helping you envision yourself as part of their vibrant tapestry.
  4. Empowering Connections: We believe that cohousing is more than just a physical space; it's about fostering meaningful connections with like-minded individuals. Our website is not just a platform for finding a home but also a hub for fostering connections within the cohousing community. Through forums, events, and networking opportunities, we aim to facilitate interactions that go beyond the digital realm, ultimately strengthening the cohousing movement as a whole.
  5. Continual Support: Our commitment to you doesn't end once you've found your cohousing community. We are here to support you throughout your journey, providing resources, articles, and guides that address common challenges, offer insights into community dynamics, and promote personal growth within the cohousing lifestyle. We strive to be a trusted resource and a catalyst for positive change in your cohousing experience.

Join Our Cohousing Community

We invite you to embark on a journey of discovery, connection, and purpose by exploring our website and connecting with cohousing communities that resonate with your dreams. Whether you are seeking a peaceful retreat, an intergenerational haven, or an eco-conscious hub, our website is your gateway to finding the cohousing community that feels like home.

Welcome to Cohouse, where we are redefining the way you find your ideal cohousing home and helping you build a life enriched by shared experiences, genuine connections, and the joy of belonging.
